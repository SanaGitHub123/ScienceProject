Why do we need Valencies?

Valency is crucial for maintaining chemical stability in atoms. It determines the number of bonds an atom can form with other atoms. Valency is related to an atom's electron configuration. It provides information about the distribution of electrons in different energy levels and helps understand the overall electronic structure of elements.
